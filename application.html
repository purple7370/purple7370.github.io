Infiltrating the Airship

Information

Infiltrating the Airship is the fourth installment of the Henry Stickmin series, following the titular character on his new mission. After breaking into a bank, escaping a prison, and stealing a diamond, he's been captured by the government and tasked with a new mission: find the secrets of the Toppat clan. However, by the discretion of the player, Henry has his own plans for this mission.

How to play

This is a simple point-and-click choose-your-own-adventure game. Use the left mouse button to select scenarios that appear to change the course of the story.

  1. When the game starts, you are going to be shown a scene explaining the scenario the character is in. Afterwards, you will be given four options to choose from: Earpiece, Cannon Ball, Grapple Gun, and Sticky Hand. Each choice will open up a new path that will change how the story unfolds.

  2. After selecting something, a new scene plays out, presenting the player with another set of options, and the process is repeated. Be careful though! Some options are timed.

  3. Sometimes, choosing an option will result in a fail. There are 60 unique fails in the game, and 4 endings.

  4. Continue repeating these steps until you have explored every route, found every fail and every ending, and unlocked every achievement!
