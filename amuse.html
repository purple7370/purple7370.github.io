Google Easter Eggs

Google is well known for being the most popular search engine, but it's also popular for having a ton of easter eggs hidden around depending on what is searched, and two interesting ones are described below.

Blink

Blink is the first easter egg I would like to point out. Referencing a now ignored HTML tag, it causes every instance of the word 'blink' to blink. It can be accessed by searching for "<blink>", "blink tag", and "blink html". Using either of the last two will also cause every instance of the words "tag" or "html" to blink, respectively.

Click here to see the easter egg in action!

Askew

Askew is the second easter egg I would like to point out. Searching for this causes the screen to be at a permanent tilt. It is not the only search that will tilt the screen, as other searches like "tipsy" and "do a barrel roll" will also create a similar effect, albeit not permanently.
